Welcome to Clique AV

The strategies chosen by Clique AV are made operational through various initiatives that aim at internationalization of curriculum, enabling graduates to acquire defining skills for competing in a global knowledge society.



Today, there are more methods of accessing higher education than ever. Students can earn a degree or diploma online or in person, full-time or part-time, overseas, or in a native country. This diversification increases competition among colleges & universities.



We believe there exists an established need in the higher education space to help discover the 'niche', through building “Centre of Excellence” in teaching, research, and extension education. Clique AV offers student and faculty engagement through various training and syllabi design, stipend-based projects, internships, and placement.
